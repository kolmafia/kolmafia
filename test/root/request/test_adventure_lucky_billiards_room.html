A Shark's Chum

As you walk into the Billiard room, you see an angry-looking spectre drifting around the table. It spots you and begins stalking toward you, hissing "Sharked! Hustled! Cheated!"

"Sounds like someone's a sore loser," you smirk.

The ghost howls with rage and rushes at you. Looks like this is your cue, if you've got the balls.

You suddenly feel less lucky.
