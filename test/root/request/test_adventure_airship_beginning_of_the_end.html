The Beginning of the End of the Penultimate Fantasy

You step off the beanstalk onto the Airship, and are immediately confronted by one of the grunts in charge of keeping the propellers wound up. "Where do you think you're going?" he snarls.

"Uh. . ." you spit out the first lame lie that comes to mind, "I just woke up on a tropical beach with amnesia and I made my way here."

"Oh, well, that sounds about right. You're probably going to be the protagonist of this story, then. So you might as well know that we're looking for the Four Immateria, a quartet of insubstantial elements that will allow us to construct an airship to get to the Castle in the Clouds in the Sky."

"So you're up here in your airship looking for parts to build an . . . airship?"

"Yup!" the grunt replies. "There's something about dark spirits and some aliens and stuff, and punching through planets and whatnot, but that's about the size of it."

"Well, I guess I'll keep an eye out for those immateria, then, so we can get off this airship and onto an airship," you say, cocking your head at the guy, still not sure he's not making fun of you.

"Sounds good!" the grunt nods and goes back to winding the propellers.
