Boxing Daycare

The massive central room of the Boxing Daycare is dominated by the boxing ring, but there is a wide variety of unusual exercise equipment scattered haphazardly around the periphery. You see several heavy deltoid benches, a few weight bicep towers, and a hanging hamstring elliptical, for example. Probably around 2,875 pieces in all. 2,782 toddlers are training with an instructor in the boxing ring. It's impressive how efficient they are, what with that student-to-teacher ratio.
