"You can't bring that thing in here! It's falling apart!"

"Hey," you reply, "no he's not! He's doing just fine!"

She points to the ground. "Fine? Look at all of those pieces that just fell off of it! You call that fine? Pick those up, and get out of my Arena!"

She has a point. Which piece would you like to pick up first?
