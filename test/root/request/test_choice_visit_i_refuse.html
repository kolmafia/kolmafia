I Refuse!

You're walking between huge piles of foul-smelling garbage, looking for something to beat up, when all of a sudden you're completely overwhelmed by the stench of this place.

Like, seriously. You've got to get out of here. Right now.

You lose control of your legs, and begin running as fast as you can in a random direction. Unfortunately for you, that random direction seems to have taken you over a weak spot in the ground, as you plunge through and into a darkened underground chamber.

You light a match and look around, and to your surprise, find yourself in a relatively clean chamber. Clean, but cluttered -- in fact, the walls aren't actually walls, they're just piles of miscellaneous junk. It's much drier down here than on the surface, though -- it hardly stinks at all. You could stand to hang out down here for a while, if you wanted to pick through the junk and see if you can find anything worth keeping.
