Moon Over the Dark Heart

As you search the Dark Heart of the Woods (you're somewhere in the dark left ventricle at the moment), you almost trip over a little imp who was carrying a big plate of hot wings.

"Hey, watch where you're going, you big undamned galoot!" he shouts.

"Sorry," you say. "Where were you going with those wings?"

"I'm having a party with my fellow imps at our secret treasure stash," he says. "We just stole something from those tubby friars, and I can't wait to show it off!"

"I'll bet you didn't really steal from the friars. Prove it!" you say, craftily.

"No way, loser!" the imp says, and leaps away, dropping the plate of wings. He pauses just long enough to moon you before vanishing into the brush.

Well, it appears you've reached an impasse.

The wings got all dirty when he dropped them, but the plate looks interesting -- you pocket it. Er, you sack it, since it's too big to pocket.

You acquire an item: hot plate
